[Classical]

CULTURAL SCHISM - Ideological factions within society clash sharply.
Building projects become symbols of factional rivalry.

TECHNOLOGICAL BREAKTHROUGH - Innovations in engineering, science, or philosophy emerge.
Infrastructure accidents damage public trust.

[Medieval]

[Modern]

POPULATION SURGE - Urbanisation accelerates, straining infrastructure.
Expanded housing, transport, and sanitation stabilise growth.

ECONOMIC BOOM - Industrial output, global trade, or tech investment surges.
Corruption and mismanagement waste the economic surge.

[Space]

LEADERSHIP CRISIS - Political divisions form between planetary governors, fleet command, and central authority.
Reforms strengthen command chains and planetary governance.

CULTURAL SCHISM - Divergence between planet-born citizens, station-dwellers, and colony cultures fractures unity.
Shared interplanetary academies and cultural hubs strengthen unity.

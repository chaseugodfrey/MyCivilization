INTERNAL CONFLICT - Tensions rise between clans due to disputes over hunting grounds, resources, or personal grievances. Without intervention, violence may erupt.
Host a mediation circle where clan leaders speak openly.
Leaders gather around the central fire, voicing frustrations and misunderstandings. Through patient dialogue, they rediscover shared goals and agree to renewed cooperation.

ECONOMIC BOOM - A sudden abundance of valuable resources brings prosperity, but risks pride, waste, and tension between clans.
Send settlers to explore and expand into newly promising regions.
The festival grows excessive, and accusations arise that some clans contributed less or took more. What should have united instead stirs envy and bitterness.

DISEASE OUTBREAK — Illness sweeps through crowded cities and trade ports. The state must act quickly to contain panic and loss.
Organise communal prayer gatherings and rites for health.
Physicians coordinate treatments in safe, isolated wards. Though harsh, the containment slows the spread and reassures citizens that the state is acting decisively.

CULTURAL SCHISM — Philosophers, generals, priests, and merchants clash over values and the direction of the state. Ideological divides threaten the harmony of the republic or kingdom.
Commission respected philosophers to write doctrines promoting unity.
The forum becomes a celebration of reasoned discourse. Citizens feel heard, tempers ease, and the shared pursuit of knowledge rekindles unity across factions.

Publish the breakthrough openly to accelerate global scientific collaboration.
International labs build upon the discovery rapidly. Your nation becomes synonymous with scientific leadership, and global goodwill increases.

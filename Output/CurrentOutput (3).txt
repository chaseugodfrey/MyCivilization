NATURAL DISASTER - A violent storm, flood, drought, or earth-shaking event disrupts the tribe’s daily life, threatening safety and survival.
Divide the tribe into task-groups to protect food, homes, and children.
Despite desperate effort, the shelters collapse under the storm’s fury. Injuries mount and valuable supplies are lost, deepening fear and exposing the tribe’s vulnerability.

MORAL DILEMMA - A difficult ethical choice confronts the tribe, forcing clans to weigh tradition, survival, compassion, and long-term consequences.
Refuse help to outsiders and strengthen borders to protect your own.
The outsider brings hidden danger — sickness, a vengeful clan, or treachery. Your goodwill backfires as the tribe pays the price for trusting an unknown stranger.

LEADERSHIP CRISIS — Senators, generals, or governors challenge central authority. Power struggles threaten stability and endanger the unity of the classical state.
Establish a council of philosophers to guide decision-making.
The senate erupts into chaos as factions accuse each other of treason. Negotiations fail, and the public witnesses a humiliating collapse of political order.

CULTURAL SCHISM — Philosophers, generals, priests, and merchants clash over values and the direction of the state. Ideological divides threaten the harmony of the republic or kingdom.
Hold athletic festivals to shift focus from ideology to shared civic pride.
The forum spirals into shouting and personal attacks. Factions leave more entrenched than before, and the forum becomes a symbol of civic dysfunction.

RESOURCE SHORTAGE — A failed harvest, disrupted caravan, or mismanaged storehouse has led to widespread scarcity. Grain prices rise, villages stir with unease, and whispers of misrule test the kingdom's stability.
Request emergency grain shipments from neighbouring kingdoms through noble diplomacy.
The inspection uncovers hidden stockpiles illegally held by corrupt local officials. Their seizure restores fairness, and villagers celebrate the kingdom’s vigilance. Order returns as supplies are redistributed.

MORAL DILEMMA — A controversial case of justice, heresy, or peasant revolt forces the realm to choose between strict order and mercy. The decision will echo through legend.
Defer the decision and send the accused on a religious pilgrimage as penance.
The trial devolves into a spectacle of outrage. Shouting drowns out reason, and the final judgment satisfies no one, deepening division.

MORAL DILEMMA — A controversial legal case, whistleblower leak, or viral injustice forces the nation to confront questions of ethics, justice, and social stability.
Establish an independent ethics committee to review the case.
The hearing becomes politicised, with grandstanding overshadowing substance. Public frustration intensifies as real answers fail to emerge.

RESOURCE SHORTAGE — A supply crisis hits the nation as fuel, materials, or essential goods run scarce due to embargoes, industrial failures, or global disruption. Factories stall, transport slows, and citizens grow anxious.
Divert military logistics and transport fleets to support civilian supply distribution.
Public frustration boils over as long queues and blackouts spread. Social media amplifies complaints, and protests erupt demanding government accountability. Order becomes harder to maintain.

DISEASE OUTBREAK — A mutated alien microbe or synthetic pathogen escapes containment, threatening multiple worlds.
Deploy AI‑guided nanomedicine teams to outbreak zones.
The rings halt interplanetary transmission. Doctors gain crucial time to develop treatments, saving millions.

NATURAL DISASTER — A solar flare surge, terraforming failure, asteroid strike warning, or alien microbe outbreak threatens the stability of one or more colonies.
Mobilise scientific task forces to study the event and prevent recurrence.
The evacuation proceeds with precision. Millions are relocated safely, and gratitude strengthens interplanetary unity.
